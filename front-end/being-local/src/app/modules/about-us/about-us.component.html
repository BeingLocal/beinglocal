Our Story

Being Local is started with a mission 'Every Step Counts', every step we take towards building awareness of Indian Products.

We believe that Being Local will be a one stop destination for all consumers to know the manufacturers of the Product, and for retailers to identify the products that needs to be put in the premium shelf space.

With the movement of #SelfReliantIndia we realized that the leaders of the country were easily able to tap on the sentiment and mobilize the crowds to think about the products they are using. However, when we tried to search for the information there was no single point of destination to find that information. That was the moment we decided to build an app to list the information.

We also believe that this listing will help us identify the markets we are yet to explore and make a space.

Being Local is built by three passionate Indians who wants to do their bit for their country with LinkedIn profile
